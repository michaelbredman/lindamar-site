Laundry 101

Tips for a Great Wash

Get the most out of every visit with these quick tips.

üß∫

Don't Overload

Fill the drum about three-quarters full. Clothes need room to tumble for a thorough, even clean.

üå°Ô∏è

Sort by Temperature

Whites in hot, colors in warm, delicates in cold. Sorting prevents fading and fabric damage.

Use HE Detergent

High-Efficiency detergent is recommended for our machines. A little goes a long way.

Size Your Load Right

Use 20 lb washers for small loads and the 80 lb washer for king-size bedding. Right-sizing saves money.
